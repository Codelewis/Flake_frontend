About Us

Flake is a micro business platform for unemployed youth. Flake aimes to provide an online space for young Kenyans to showcase their skills, products and portfolios and have themselves booked and hired for their services and products in real time. It is our goal to promote financial inclusion through self-employment while providing business development.
